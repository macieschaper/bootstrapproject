Grinnell Glacier Lake
Grinnell Glacier – 10.3mi / 16.5km
Grinnell Glacier is the most frequently visited glacier in Glacier National Park with an incredible trail along the way. The first two level miles are spent briefly along Swiftcurrent Lake and Lake Josephine’s north shores.Once at the end of the lakes, the elevation begins. From here on out, the views keep getting better as you climb with increased chances of seeing the area’s big game.
